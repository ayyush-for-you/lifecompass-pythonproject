# 🌐 LifeCompass – AI-Powered Career & Life Guidance Platform

## 📌 Overview

**LifeCompass** is an intelligent web-based platform designed to solve **Information Overload**, **Decision Fatigue**, and **Career Confusion** among students, professionals, and career switchers.

Unlike traditional career guidance systems that only focus on conventional careers, LifeCompass explores **creative, technical, sports, business, and interdisciplinary career paths** based on a user's:

* Interests
* Hobbies
* Personality
* Skills
* Emotional state
* Lifestyle balance

The platform combines **career guidance, mental wellness, skill analysis, productivity tracking, and AI mentorship** into one ecosystem.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of students and professionals struggle with:

* Confusion after 12th
* Pressure to choose “safe” careers
* Burnout from academics/jobs
* Losing touch with hobbies and passions
* Lack of personalized career guidance
* Fear of unconventional career paths
* Career switching anxiety

LifeCompass addresses these problems through structured guidance, realistic planning, and AI-powered recommendations.

# ⚙️ How It Works

1. User selects a mode
2. Completes assessment questions
3. Recommendation engine analyzes:

   * Interests
   * Personality
   * Skills
   * Stress level
4. AI system generates:

   * Career recommendations
   * Balance suggestions
   * Skill roadmap
5. Dashboard displays:

   * Career match %
   * Progress graphs
   * Personalized plans
